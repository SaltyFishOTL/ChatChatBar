```markdown
# <Project> Handoff

Last updated: YYYY-MM-DD
Branch/worktree:
Latest stable commit:
Purpose:

## Completed
- Current facts only, with evidence.

## In Progress
- One active slice.

## Tried And Failed
- Failed attempts future agents should not repeat.

## Tried And Not Adopted
- Viable options rejected, with short reasons.

## Untested
- Flows with no strong evidence.

## Unconfirmed
- Open facts or product decisions.

## Blockers
- Hard blockers and soft risks.

## Recommended Next Steps
1. Next concrete action.

## Architecture Notes
- Ownership boundaries and maintainability guardrails.

## Verification Baseline
- Latest meaningful commands/results/device evidence.
```
